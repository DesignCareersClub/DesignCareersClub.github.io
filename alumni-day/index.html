We have invited various UC Davis Design alumni from different Design fields to discuss their profession and allow students to engage and ask questions.

FAQs

How do I sign up?

Sign up for these events by filling out a Google form, and you will be sent the event’s Zoom link an hour before the event starts! They must be submitted by 5:00 PM the day before the event. If you sign up after 5pm, you must email us at designcareersclub@gmail.com

What can I expect to gain?

If you have an interest in the tech, creative agency, or nonprofit industry, come learn about alumni’s experience in their careers and ask your questions!

Is there a fee?

This event is free to all students! Take advantage of these connections and alumni’s time taken to share their experience.
