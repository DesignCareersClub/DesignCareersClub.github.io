about us

The UC Davis Design Careers Club aims to empower students to take risks not only in design but in career aspirations. We're a student-run organization building a better campus to support our diverse students and creative community.

connection
inclusion
guidance

We serve the design community because we believe we can help each other as students. We seek to foster communication and connection with one another.

Our club is an open resource to all design students at Davis. We believe that success should be inclusive and we structure our events accordingly.

We want to help designers reach their personal and professional goals. We support students by providing extracurricular workshops, resources, and other guidance.
