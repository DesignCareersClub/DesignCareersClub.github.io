February 5th

Design Careers Fair

Learn about design internship and job opportunities, hear design professionals share their stories, and network with recruiters, professors, and fellow students!

The 2021 Design Careers Fair will take place on Friday Feb. 5th, and will be a virtual event hosted on Remo. In the morning session, we will have guest speakers from the industry talk about their field of expertise. During the afternoon session, students will have the chance to network with company representatives and have their portfolios and resumes reviewed.

Morning speaker session: 10:00 to 11:30 AM
Afternoon company session: 1:00 to 4:00 PM
note: it is not mandatory that you attend the full event.
