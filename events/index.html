Unmute and Unwind - Part 1

Fri January 21

3:00pm pst

Zoom

In this workshop with Trace Manuel, we’ll explore burnout on a number of levels: from how it manifests in our tissues and nerves to how it shows up in our lives and creative practices. Together, we’ll practice accessible skills to reflect upon, recognize, and ultimately regulate creative burnout in a way that’s individualized and actionable. Get cozy, bring something to doodle with, and get ready to feel (less of) the burn!
